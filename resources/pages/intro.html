Founded in 1994 in Taipei, Intradin was established by Mr. Feng Guang yuan. The Chinese name of Intradin stands for “an excellent scholar will be engaged in business”.

At Intradin’s inception, commerce and trade across the Taiwan Strait was just beginning. China soon became Intradin’s main trade and customer service base. Intradin has set up three manufacturing bases respectively in Shanghai, Huzhou and Qingdao. The Company management system has respectively passed the certification of ISO9001 Quality Management System, OHSAS18001 Occupational Health & Safety Management System and ISO14001 Environment Management System. 

Up to today, Intradin has grown from a trade novice in the hardware industry into an enterprise that fully integrates industrial manufacturing of hose & cord reel, lubrication equipment, fuel transfer equipment, hydraulic products and meat processor etc. Intradin with its expertise in these specialties its independent R&D team, and its lean manufacturing ability, has developed into one of China’s best tool and equipment supplier.

Over the past two decades, Intradin has won recognition from its customers, suppliers, employees, professional certification institutions and relevant government departments.  Intradin has continually and consistently served customers from more than 70 countries and regions.

Intradin’s values are Integrity, Practicality, Proactivity, and Lifelong Learning”. Intradin energetic and innovative team is characterized by diligence, ambition, specialty and confidence. These are Intradin’s strengths, and also our foundation on which we provide customers with excellent service.
